|LCT=yes}}The oldest dataflow programming language. It belongs to the [https://www.CS.CMU.edu/~crary/819-f09/Landin66.pdf <abbr title="If you See What I Mean">ISWIM</abbr>] family of languages. The computations are expressed as manipulations of sequences (much like the [[Unix pipes]] model of computation.).
