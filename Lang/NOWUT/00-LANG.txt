The NOWUT compiler is a free/[[open source]] ([[GPL]]), 32-bit, self-hosted implementation of the NOWUT language for [[Microsoft]] [[Windows]], [[DOS]], [[Amiga]], and [[X68000]], with additional support for [[SuperH]] as a target.
