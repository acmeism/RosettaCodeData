'''Nascom BASIC''' is the collective name for the several versions of [[:Category:BASIC|BASIC]] for [[Nascom]] 8-bit computers.

Besides Nascom BASIC, there exist other versions of BASIC ported to Nascom computers, e.g. [[:Category:Tiny BASIC|Tiny BASIC]] (version of [[Palo Alto Tiny BASIC]]), [[:Category:Hisoft BASIC|Hisoft BASIC]], or XTAL BASIC.
