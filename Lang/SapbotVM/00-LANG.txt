SapbotVM is easy-to-implement virtual machine architecture, made by Romarchive, that's not compilable, but only have interpreted Assembler. Also, this language have two formats: SVM1, SVM2. First, is based on JSON, second is custom.

SapbotVM have limited support for monitor, no internet and plug and play devices. So only serial port.
