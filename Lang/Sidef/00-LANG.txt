Sidef is a modern, expressive programming language that combines the elegance of [[derived from::Ruby]], the versatility of [[derived from::Raku]], and the mathematical power of a built-in computer algebra system.

The language is mostly used as a research programming language.

For more details, see: [https://github.com/trizen/sidef https://github.com/trizen/sidef]
