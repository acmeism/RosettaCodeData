'''Wisp''' is a dialect of [[Scheme]] that replaces outer parentheses by indentation while adhering to the minimalist spirit of Scheme. It is standardized in [https://srfi.schemers.org/srfi-119/srfi-119.html SRFI-119]. The main [https://www.draketo.de/software/wisp implementation] of Wisp uses [[Guile]] as platform.
