An [[wp:Anadrome|anadrome]] is similar to a [[Palindrome_detection| palindrome]] except, rather than spelling the ''same'' word or phrase when reversed, it spells a ''different'' word or phrase. An anadrome is a special case of an [[Anagrams|anagram]].

''Anadrome is a [[wp:Portmanteau|portmanteau]] of the words anagram and palindrome.''

For instance, '''regal''' and '''lager''' are anadromes.
