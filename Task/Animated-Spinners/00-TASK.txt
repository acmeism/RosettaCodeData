;Task:
Create and display five spinners.
One spinner in the middle and four spinners surrounding it.
Each spinner is created by drawing radius lines around a center axis and then looping through the drawing to simulate a moving clock hand.
When the loop is sped up, the illusion of a spinner is created.
NOTE: The animated GIF example is actually slower than what you should be able to create. A fast animation will fill in more radial lines with a pleasing appearance.

Extra credit for offsetting the spinners with mouse movement.
