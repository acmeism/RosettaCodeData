Dividuus numbers are integers that are divisible by the sum of their digits, the product of their digits, their digital root, and their multiplicative digital root.


'''''Dividuus''' is Latin for 'divisible'.''


;Task
* Find and display the first fifty dividuus numbers.


;Stretch
* Find and display the dividuus numbers between 990 million and one billion.
