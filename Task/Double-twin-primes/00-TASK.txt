'''Definition'''
<br>
Let (p1,p2) and (p3,p4) be twin primes where p3 - p2 = 4.
<br>
Such primes called '''Double Twin Primes'''
<br> <br>
'''Example'''
<br>
[5,7,11,13]
<br>
'''Task'''
<br>
Find and show here all Double Twin Primes under 1000.
