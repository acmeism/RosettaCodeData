;Definition
'''n''' &nbsp; is an &nbsp; ''extra prime'' &nbsp; if &nbsp; '''n''' &nbsp; is prime and its decimal digits and sum of digits are also primes.


;Task
Show the extra primes under &nbsp; '''10,000'''.
