;Task:
Given a list of integer arrays '''nums''' (which may be unsorted), find the smallest missing positive integer in each array in the list.

A missing positive integer is any positive integer, starting from 1, which is not present in the array.


;Example:
* '''nums'''&nbsp; = &nbsp; [1,2,0], [3,4,-1,1], [7,8,9,11,12]
* '''output''' = &nbsp; 3, 2, 1
