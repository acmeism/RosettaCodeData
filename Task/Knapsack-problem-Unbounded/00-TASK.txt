A traveler gets diverted and has to make an unscheduled stop in what turns out to be Shangri La. &nbsp; Opting to leave, he is allowed to take as much as he likes of the following items, so long as it will fit in his knapsack, and he can carry it.

He knows that he can carry no more than &nbsp; 25 &nbsp; 'weights' in total; &nbsp; and that the capacity of his knapsack is &nbsp; 0.25 &nbsp; 'cubic lengths'.

Looking just above the bar codes on the items he finds their weights and volumes. &nbsp; He digs out his recent copy of a  financial paper and gets the value of each item.
