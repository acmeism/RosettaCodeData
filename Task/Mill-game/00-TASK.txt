;Task:
Create an implementation of the classic [https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Nine_men%27s_morris Mill game].
<br><br>
The game is also known as Nine Men's Morris, Mill, Mills, Merels, Merelles y Merrills.
<br>
In Spanish, it is known as juego del molino or molino; in French, as jeu du moulin; and in Piedmontese, as grisha.
<br>
Is an ancient tabletop board game for two players. The aim of the game is to remove your opponent's game pieces (called "men") one at a time, by moving 3 of your own "men" into a row (called a "mill"). 
<br>
Each "mill" you make gives you the power to select one of your opponent's men for removal. 
<br>
While moving your pieces, you can use tactics and strategy to block your opponent from building his own "mill".
