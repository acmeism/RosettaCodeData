Similar to [[Prime groups]], except you're asked to return a count of all of the unique prime groupings of the input string.

* A prime grouping is a grouping of <code>n</code> characters from a string, where all pairs of its absolute differences between the characters' character codes are primes.

Here, <code>a</code> is defined as the largest number through which a prime group with <code>n</code> characters may be found.

Specifically:
* Input a string from the user.
* Print a number:
:* the count of all prime groupings in that string, from <code>2</code> to <code>a</code>.
