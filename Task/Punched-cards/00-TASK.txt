Your very distant relative left you an inheritance of a slightly dusty yet fully functional IBM 360 machine. Unfortunately the greedy government forced you to sell the card puncher to pay off the inheritance tax. What should you do? You decided to pick through the holes by hand. Now you need a template. So your task is to print punched cards. Luckily there are documents left. See [https://dl.acm.org/doi/pdf/10.1145/362991.363052 the Hollerith punched card code].

You can omit non-graphic characters and the IBM zero with the dot inside. As a test print the punched cards of the hello-world program in your language.
