The matrix can be stored in any datatype that is convenient

Built-in functions or this pseudocode (from Wikipedia) may be used:

             '''stop'''
         '''end if'''

             ''i'' = ''i'' + 1

                 '''end if'''
             '''end if'''
         '''end while'''

         If M[''r'', ''lead''] is not 0 divide row ''r'' by M[''r'', ''lead'']
