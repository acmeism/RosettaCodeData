An &nbsp; IQ Puzzle &nbsp; is a triangle of 15 golf tee's. 


This puzzle is typically seen at Cracker Barrel &nbsp; (a USA sales store) &nbsp; where one tee is missing and the remaining tees jump over each other &nbsp; (with removal of the jumped tee, like checkers) &nbsp; until one tee is left. 

The fewer tees left, &nbsp; the higher the IQ score. 

Peg &nbsp; #1 &nbsp; is the top centre through to the bottom row which are pegs 11 through to 15.
